A Message from the Principal

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-Wishers,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to ABC Public School — an institution that I have had the privilege of leading for the past decade. When I first walked through these doors, I saw not just a school, but a place of endless possibility — a space where young minds could be shaped, nurtured, and inspired to achieve greatness.

"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire." — W.B. Yeats

At ABC Public School, we believe deeply in this philosophy. Our goal has never been to merely prepare students for examinations — though we are proud of our 100% board results for five consecutive years. Our true mission is to ignite a lifelong love of learning, to develop critical thinkers, compassionate human beings, and responsible citizens who can contribute meaningfully to society.

We are privileged to have a faculty of exceptionally dedicated educators who bring passion, expertise, and genuine care to their classrooms every single day. They go beyond the textbook to connect with each child individually, understanding their unique strengths, learning styles, and personal challenges. This personalized approach is at the heart of what makes ABC Public School different.

To our students, I want to say this: Your years here are among the most formative of your life. Embrace every opportunity — academic and otherwise. Join the sports team. Participate in debates. Explore the science lab. Read beyond your syllabus. Make friends. Make mistakes. Learn from them. And never, ever stop asking questions. Curiosity is your greatest asset.

To parents and guardians: You are our most important partners. The values you instill at home, combined with the education we provide, create a foundation that your child will build upon for a lifetime. We thank you for entrusting us with your child's most precious years, and we pledge to honour that trust every single day.

As we move forward, we remain committed to our vision of academic excellence combined with character building. We will continue to invest in better infrastructure, better training for our teachers, and better opportunities for our students — because your children deserve nothing less than the best.

With warm regards and best wishes for your success,

Mrs. Sunita Sharma

Principal, ABC Public School, Varanasi
