A Legacy of Excellence
in Education

Founded in 1995, ABC Public School has been a beacon of quality education in Varanasi for nearly three decades. Our institution is built on the foundation of academic excellence, character building, and holistic development of every student.

As a CBSE-affiliated school, we follow a curriculum that prepares students for national-level competitive examinations while nurturing their individual talents and creativity. Our dedicated faculty of 50+ experienced educators ensures personalized attention to every learner.

We believe that true education goes beyond textbooks — it shapes leaders, builds character, and prepares students for the challenges of tomorrow's world.
