Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to secure admission for your child at ABC Public School.

1

Fill Online Enquiry Form

Complete the admission enquiry form on our website or visit the school office to collect and submit the enquiry form.

2

Receive Call from Admissions Team

Our admissions counsellor will contact you within 24 working hours to guide you through the next steps.

3

Visit School & Collect Form

Visit our campus between 8:00 AM – 2:00 PM (Monday to Saturday) to collect the official admission form and experience our facilities firsthand.

4

Submit Documents

Submit the duly filled admission form along with all required documents at the school's admission counter.

5

Interaction & Registration

The student will attend a brief interaction session (age-appropriate). Upon successful interaction, registration is confirmed.

6

Fee Payment & Confirmation

Pay the applicable fees (online or at the school office) to complete the admission process. You will receive a confirmation letter and admission kit.
