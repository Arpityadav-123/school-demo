Our Facilities

Home›Facilities

World-Class Infrastructure

At ABC Public School, we believe that the right environment is essential for quality education. Our modern campus, spread across 8,500 square meters, is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities designed to inspire, engage, and support every learner.

🖥️

Computer Lab

Our fully air-conditioned computer lab houses 60 modern systems with high-speed fiber internet connectivity. Students from Class III onwards learn digital literacy, MS Office, and basic programming. Senior students have access to advanced coding tools and software for Science and Commerce projects.

  • 60 computers with i5 processors
  • 100 Mbps dedicated fiber internet
  • Licensed educational software
  • 2 dedicated ICT teachers
🔬

Science Laboratories

We have three fully equipped, separate laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology — giving students dedicated spaces for subject-specific experiments without any scheduling conflicts.

  • Physics Lab — instruments & mechanics equipment
  • Chemistry Lab — fume hood, reagent storage
  • Biology Lab — microscopes, specimen displays
  • All labs meet CBSE safety standards
📚

Library

Our library is a treasure house of knowledge with over 8,000 books spanning academic textbooks, reference books, fiction, non-fiction, magazines, newspapers, and encyclopedias. A digital catalogue system makes it easy to find any resource.

  • 8,000+ books and 500+ e-resources
  • Dedicated reading room with 50 seats
  • Monthly book exhibitions
  • Separate children's reading corner
⚽

Sports Ground

Our sprawling 4,000 sqm sports ground provides ample space for a wide range of sports activities. Students are encouraged to participate in at least one sport as part of their holistic development.

  • Cricket pitch with matting
  • Football ground (FIFA standard dimensions)
  • Basketball court (concrete, with markings)
  • Athletics track for running events
  • Badminton and Volleyball courts
🚌

Transport Facility

Our school operates a fleet of 12 GPS-enabled school buses covering 15 routes across Varanasi and surrounding areas, ensuring safe and timely commute for students.

  • 12 buses, 15 routes across Varanasi
  • GPS tracking — real-time location
  • Trained drivers and a lady attendant per bus
  • First aid kit in every vehicle
  • CCTV camera in every bus
📺

Smart Classrooms

Every one of our 42 classrooms is equipped with projectors and interactive digital boards, transforming traditional teaching into an engaging, multimedia learning experience.

  • 42 smart classrooms with HD projectors
  • Interactive whiteboards with touch function
  • Digital content linked to CBSE curriculum
  • WiFi connectivity in all classrooms
  • Regular teacher training on EdTech tools

Additional Amenities

🏥

Medical Room

A fully equipped sick room with a trained nurse on duty. Emergency first aid and basic medical care are available during school hours.

🎨

Art & Craft Room

A dedicated creative space where students explore painting, sculpture, and crafts under the guidance of qualified art teachers.

🎵

Music Room

Equipped with keyboard, tabla, harmonium, and guitar — students can learn classical and contemporary music from Class I onwards.

🔒

CCTV Surveillance

Over 60 CCTV cameras cover the entire campus including classrooms, corridors, parking areas, and the main gate for 24/7 safety.

💧

RO Drinking Water

RO-purified drinking water is available throughout the campus at every floor. Water quality is tested regularly.

♿

Ramps for Disabled

Our campus is disability-friendly with ramps, accessible toilets, and ground-floor classrooms available for students with special needs.
