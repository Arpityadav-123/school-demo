Our Story

ABC Public School was established in 1995 by the ABC Education Society with a vision to provide quality, affordable education to the children of Varanasi and surrounding areas. What started as a small school with just 3 classrooms and 80 students has today grown into one of the most respected CBSE-affiliated institutions in eastern Uttar Pradesh.

Over three decades, we have nurtured thousands of students who have gone on to become doctors, engineers, civil servants, entrepreneurs, and leaders in various fields. Our alumni are our greatest achievement and our strongest ambassadors.

Today, ABC Public School stands tall with a modern campus spanning 8,500 square meters, 42 classrooms, state-of-the-art laboratories, a rich library, and a dedicated sports ground — all designed to provide the best learning environment for our 1,200+ students.

Our Journey Through Time

🌱 1995 — The Beginning

ABC Public School was founded by the ABC Education Society. Started with Class Nursery to V, 80 students, and a dedicated team of 8 teachers committed to quality education.

📈 2003 — Expansion

The school expanded to include Classes VI–X and received CBSE affiliation (No. 2130456). Student strength crossed 400 and a new building block was constructed.

🎓 2010 — Senior Secondary

Senior Secondary classes (XI–XII) with Science and Commerce streams were added. The school achieved 100% board result for the first time, setting a tradition of excellence.

🏆 2024 — Legacy of Excellence

Over 1,200 students, 50+ faculty members, 42 smart classrooms, and 5 consecutive years of 100% board results. Recognized as one of the top schools in eastern UP.

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To provide a stimulating, nurturing, and inclusive learning environment where every student is inspired to achieve academic excellence, develop strong moral values, and become a responsible, compassionate citizen of India.

We are committed to partnering with families to support each child's unique journey of growth — intellectually, emotionally, physically, and socially.

🌟 Our Vision

To be the most trusted educational institution in Varanasi — one that is recognized not just for academic results, but for the quality of character it builds in its students.

We envision graduates who are confident, creative, ethical, and globally aware — individuals ready to lead and serve in whatever field they choose.
